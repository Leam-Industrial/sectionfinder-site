Why SectionFinder

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Built for the workshop floor, the drawing office, and everywhere in between.

Instant Search
Find any section by dimension in seconds. Type 203x133 and get exactly what you need — no scrolling, no guessing.
Full Structural Data
Second moments of area, section moduli, torsional properties, warping constants, plastic resistance values — the complete picture.
Cross-Section Diagrams
Annotated technical diagrams for every section family. See exactly what h, b, tw, tf and r1 mean on the actual profile.
